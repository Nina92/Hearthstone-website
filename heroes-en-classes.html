Heroes & Classes

Er zijn 9 verschillende classes die je kunt spelen in Hearthstone met ieder hun eigen hero. Elke hero heeft zijn eigen unieke hero power.

Hieronder staan alle hero's met hun bijbehorende class en hero power.

Class: Druid
Hero power: Shapeshift (+1 Attack this turn +1 Armor)
Class: Hunter
Hero power: Steady shot (Deal 2 damage to the enemy hero)
Class: Mage
Hero power: Fireblast (Deal 1 damage to a character)
Class: Paladin
Hero power: Reinforce (Summon a 1/1 Silver Hand Recruit)
Class: Priest
Hero power: Lesser Heal (Restore 2 Health)
Class: Rogue
Hero power: Dagger Mastery (Equip a 1/2 Dagger)
Class: Shaman
Hero power: Totemic Call (Summon a random Totem)
Class: Warlock
Hero power: Life Tap (Draw a card and take 2 damage)
Class: Warrior
Hero power: Armor up! (Gain 2 Armor)
